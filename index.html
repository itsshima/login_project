Online store

An online store is a digital platform where businesses and individuals can sell products or services over the internet.
Customers can browse through various categories, compare prices, read reviews, and make purchases from the
comfort of their homes. Online stores offer convenience, a wide range of products, and secure payment options.
Many e-commerce platforms use advanced technologies like artificial intelligence and data analytics to enhance the
shopping experience. With the rise of mobile commerce, businesses optimize their websites and apps to provide a
seamless and user-friendly interface for shoppers worldwide.
